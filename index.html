01. ABOUT US

Tonec Gemstones was founded by Mr. Tony as a response to the lack of transparency and irresponsible mining practices that confront the jewelry industry today. Tonec Gemstones began with the belief that exceptional quality, ethically sourced Jewelry can be an effective tool for social change. From our exceptional Jewelry designs, responsibly sourced materials, to our personalised customer service, customers don’t need to compromise between quality and consciousness. Tonec Gemstones makes jewelry you can feel good about wearing with thoughtful craftmanship in every phase of production. With our Beyond Conflict Free™ diamonds and commitment to social and environmental responsibility, we hope to inspire a more transparent, compassionate, and sustainable Jewelry industry.

02. OUR APPROACH

In our approach to creating shareholder value, we take the long-term view in considering how to deploy capital to create future earnings growth. Over the years, our strong balance sheet has afforded us the financial strength and flexibility to make significant reinvestments in our services. We accomplish this through excellent operational performance, opportune investments during industry cycles, and a disciplined approach to making acquisitions. We believe we have made wise investments not only in our services , but also in talented people and an exceptional culture of training and safety that permeates our company.
